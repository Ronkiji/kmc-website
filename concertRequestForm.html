Concert Request Form

To register your student to perform at the Kanata Music Club concerts you must submit a concert request form for each student to kmc@kanatamusicclub.com

Teachers are allowed 20 minutes of time per concert Sunday. Students are allowed two selections per concert, providing they meet the following time constraints:

  • Students grade VII and under – 4 minutes in total
  • Students grade VIII and IX – 6 minutes in total
  • Students grade X and ARCT – 10 minutes in total

In special situations when a teacher needs more than the allowed 20 minutes, it may be necessary to split the teacher’s students over two consecutive concerts, thereby keeping a good balance of studio input at each concert.

When completing the concert request form, please be sure all information is correct and complete. Please include the first initial of the composer’s name. If the repertoire listed is meant to be assessed for the graded scholarship programme please check the scholarship box. If it is general repertoire please leave the box empty. This is important because it helps keep the work of the scholarship director manageable.
