Membership

To play at our concerts students must first purchase a family membership. Family memberships need to be renewed each season. This membership entitles you and your family to attend all concerts, to play at concerts as agreed upon with your teacher. Students who plan to perform in more than one discipline will be required to hold a second memberhip for the second discipline up to a maximum of 2 membership fees per family.

Please download and print the Family Membership Application form. This should be mailed, with payment ($38.00), prior to your first performance to our Treasurer:
